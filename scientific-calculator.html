How to Use Scientific Calculator Pro

Our free online scientific calculator is designed to handle complex mathematical calculations with ease. Here's how to make the most of its features:

Basic Operations:

  • Basic Arithmetic: Use +, −, ×, ÷ for addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
  • Decimal Numbers: Click the "." button or type period on your keyboard
  • Parentheses: Use ( ) to group calculations and control order of operations
  • Clear/Backspace: Press C to clear everything or ⌫ to delete last character

Advanced Functions:

  • Trigonometry: sin(), cos(), tan() for sine, cosine, and tangent functions
  • Square Root: √() calculates the square root of a number
  • Logarithms: log() for natural logarithm calculations
  • Exponents: exp() for exponential functions or ^ for power operations
